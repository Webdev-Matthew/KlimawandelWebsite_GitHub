Reasons for the climate change

Global warming (which is a Consequence for the climate change) is due to the greenhouse effect. At first, we would like to explain which types of greenhouse effects everyone should know.

The natural greenhouse effect

This effects isn't made by humans. Without this effect, there would be no life on our planet earth. The natural greenhouse effect's job is to stop reflected sunbeams back into space after they've shined on the earth. The greenhouse layer of carbon dioxide, methane and nitrogen oxides is managing this phenomenon. Without this effect, the earth would be frozen at -18°C.

The human-caused greenhouse effect

The human-caused greenhouse effect is, as the name said, a greenhouse effect, which is not natural and made by humans. How? Simple answer: Burning fossil fuels, argriculture and land clearing are increasing the amount of greenhouse gases released into the atmosphere. The more greenhouse gases are in the atmosphere, the rarer sunbeams can be reflected back into space after shining on our earth. Thats why many humans - especially teenager -, for example the Friday's for future organisation want to send the government a message and demonstrate against the effect. In may 2019,the share in the atmosphere of CO2 is at 415 parts per million. In 2015 the share was at 400 ppm.
