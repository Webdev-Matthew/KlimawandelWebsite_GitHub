Reasons for the climate change

Global warming (which is a Consequence for the climate change) is due to the greenhouse effect. At first, we would like to explain which types of greenhouse effects everyone should know.

The natural greenhouse effect

This effects isn't made by humans. Without this effect, there would be no life on our planet earth. The natural greenhouse effect's job is to stop reflected sunbeams back into space after they've shined on the earth. The greenhouse layer of carbon dioxide, methane and nitrogen oxides is managing this phenomenon. Without this effect, the earth would be frozen at -18°C. So, now you probably ask yourself, why some countries would like to minimize this gas, which are pushed out by Cars. Thats the human-caused greenhouse effect.
